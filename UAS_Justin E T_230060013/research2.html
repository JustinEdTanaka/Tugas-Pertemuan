Can tattoos cause blood or skin cancer?

Written by Maria Cohut, Ph.D. on July 29, 2024

Some research has found a link between tattoos and an increased risk of cancer, and recent evidence appears to suggest that tattoos could heighten the risk of blood cancer, in particular. What biological mechanisms might explain this link, and should people really worry about the health implications of getting a tattoo?
